These days people prefer texting over calling. That's why one may find their phone is of message notifications whether it is Whatsapp, messenger, or the messages phone app.
The fact that many people today prefer using texting over phone calls is related to several reasons. The first reason is that people do not like the small talk and the need for an immediate reply. This means that people prefer to reply to others when they have free time and not busy with other things.
Another reasons for why it's preferable to text because even when the phone is off, the messages are still kept and saved, they will not disappear. Unlike phone calls, when the phone is off the calls will not be saved and the user will not know who has contacted him.
At the end, people of today prefer texting over phone calls because it fits their lifestyles more.

Nowadays, social media tooks too much energy, time and effort, into the way people depict their lives. They spend too much time using social media. People socialized with society, and went to social events, only to take photos and post it on social media.
Instagram is one of the most popular app, people use. According to Sheldon and Bryant (2016), they found that, the amount of time people spend on editing photos for posting on Instagram was significantly correlated with the level of narcissism, which means that, people are obsessed with their image, and how they should looks on social media. In another words, people put so much effort trying to impress another people, using the social media as a tool to achieve this.

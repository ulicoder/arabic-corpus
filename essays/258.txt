Nowadays social media is popular than ever. It differs from a person to another on how and for what they intend to use it. For some people us it to connect with each others and other people use it for publishing quotes and pictures of their days and lives.
A popular app that was discovered recently is called Instagram. Instagram is a social media app that allows people all over the world to connect with each other. Instagram gives people the opportunity to edit their photos and publish it. On the contrary to editing photos of yourself it also makes people more narcissism from spending time editing and looks at their photos. Moreover, Sheldon and Bryant (2016) found that the amount of time spend editing photos for Instagram significantly correlated with the level of narcissism.

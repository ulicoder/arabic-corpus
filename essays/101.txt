Homework is very important to a student's life. For many reasons.
Firstly, homework generally creates a sense of responsibility. They have to well plan their time and how to handle things.
Secondly, it lets the student to think by himself. This skill is highly needed to a student to solve his own problems.
Thirdly, homework review what is learned in class. Without it, student would easily forget what they learned.
Finally, yet importantly, it can create stress. This is an important necessity to a student's life. It is better to experience stress when young. Thus, when he gets in a real world, he will not get in a big trouble since he get used to it.
